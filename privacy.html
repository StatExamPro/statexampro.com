Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs your use of the software application Stat Exam Pro ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Ozzy App Dev, and of the website statexampro.com ("Website").

The Application is an educational tool designed to help students prepare for statistics exams. The Application does not require any user registration.

1. Information We Collect

The Application does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data.

When you download and use the Application, we do not require you to provide any information and we do not collect any information about you or your device automatically.

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone.

2. Third-Party Access

Since the Application does not collect any data, we do not share any data with third parties. The Application operates entirely locally on your device.

Our Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since the Application does not collect data of any kind, it is safe for users of all ages.

By using the Application, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. "Processing" means using cookies on a computer/hand held device or using or touching information in any way, including, but not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information. However, as stated above, no such processing currently occurs in the Application as no data is collected.

6. Website Analytics

The Website uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics service, to count page views and clicks on outbound store links. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not track you across sites, and does not collect personal data; we see only aggregate counts such as which pages were visited and from which country.
